Bridging the gap between responsive layouts and touch interactions. This talk tackles the unique challenges of touch devices—from handling touch events and gestures to optimizing tap targets and avoiding hover-state pitfalls. Includes practical solutions, JavaScript touch frameworks, and mobile UX patterns that enhance the experience on phones and tablets.
